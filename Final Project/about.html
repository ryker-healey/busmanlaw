About me

I was born in 1934 to a humble West Virginian Coal magnate, and have lived in our fine state my entire life. I was bereft of employment and living off my inheritiance until my coal company went bankrupt in the 1970's. With this tragic turn of events my father put me through law school and loaned me 1.4 million to get me on my feet. Through blood, sweat, and tears, I have built my own successful buisness. But I have not forgot my origins and I still fight for the every day West Virginian!
